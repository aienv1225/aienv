AI.env Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

AI.env helps users create AI-generated short videos and upload them to connected social media accounts, including TikTok.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use AI.env.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect information you provide directly, such as your email address, account connection status, and content upload settings.

When you connect a social media account, such as TikTok, we may receive access tokens, refresh tokens, account identifiers, and permission scopes authorized by you.

2. How We Use Information

We use this information to provide the service, including creating upload tasks, uploading videos, creating draft posts, checking upload status, and managing connected account access.

3. Social Media Account Access

AI.env uses connected account access only to perform actions authorized by you. We do not sell your social media account data.

4. User Content

You are responsible for the videos, images, captions, and other content you create or upload using AI.env.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share information only when required to operate the service, comply with law, prevent abuse, or interact with connected platform APIs.

6. Data Storage and Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect account tokens and user data. However, no online service can guarantee complete security.

7. Revoking Access

You can revoke AI.env access through your connected social media account settings, including TikTok account settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
